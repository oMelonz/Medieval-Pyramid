HIERARCHY PYRAMID OF MEDIEVAL EUROPE
King
The king owned all the lands. He managed peace
and the system of the country. He gave the
lands to Nobles and Lords.
Lords and Nobles
The Lords and Nobles were the next in power.
They gave land to Knights and Soldiers.
The Lords and Nobles were expected
to pay taxes to the king.
Knights and Soldiers
The Knights and Soldiers fought for
Nobles and Lords, and protected peasants.
They often lived with honour and respect
Peasants and Serfs
The peasants worked for the land of
Lords, Nobles and Knights.
They were often poor and
lived on meagre rations.
